Our Story

One brand, a complete home experience.

Osislightkleen Ltd is a Lagos-based company offering comprehensive housing solutions and professional cleaning services under one trusted roof.

We help clients find and set up apartments, design living spaces to their taste, and provide shortlet accommodation for short stays. We also deliver residential, commercial, and post-construction cleaning performed by trained professionals.

Whether you're relocating within Lagos or returning from abroad, our goal is simple: you just move in — we handle the rest.

About the CEO

Uchendu Light Zereuwa

Founder & CEO, Osislightkleen Ltd

A seasoned Lagos-based entrepreneur, passionate about solving housing problems and ensuring healthy living and working environments.

Driven by a deep appreciation for luxury homes and comfortable living spaces, Osislightkleen Ltd was born — a vision to help professionals within Nigeria and the diaspora secure quality, comfortable, and clean spaces where they can live, work, and carry out business activities peacefully.

He graduated from the Department of Economics at Abia State University, majored in Finance, and is a lover of trade. He is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN).

His business model is aimed at meeting both local and diaspora needs. He is a seasoned leader, mentor, and coach to young people — and believes in giving back to the Nigerian community, motivated by the SDGs: 1, 3, and 11.

SDG 1 — No Poverty SDG 3 — Good Health SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities
