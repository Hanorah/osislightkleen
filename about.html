Our Mission

Making relocation in Lagos effortless

At Osislightkleen Ltd, our mission is to take the stress out of moving and maintaining your space. We help clients secure apartments, design interiors to their taste, provide shortlet accommodation, and deliver professional cleaning — all under one trusted brand.

Whether you're relocating within Lagos or arriving from abroad, we believe you should simply move in and enjoy your home.

Our Vision

Lagos's trusted housing & cleaning partner

To be the go-to company in Lagos for housing solutions and professional cleaning — known for reliability, quality, and a client-first approach.

From shortlet stays for business and leisure to post-construction deep cleans, we aim to exceed expectations every time.
