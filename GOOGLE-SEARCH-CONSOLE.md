## 2. Submit the sitemap

1. Search Console → **Sitemaps**
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **Submit**

## 3. Request indexing (use www)

In **URL Inspection**, paste these exact URLs:

- https://www.osislight.com/
- https://www.osislight.com/about.html
- https://www.osislight.com/services.html
- https://www.osislight.com/contact.html

Then click **Request indexing**.

**Important:** Inspecting `https://osislight.com/` (without www) will show “URL not in property” because your property is the www version.
